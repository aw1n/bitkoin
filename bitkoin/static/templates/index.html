Why Bitcoin?

It's Fast

International wire transfers can take from a few days to more than a week, whereas Bitcoin transactions are generally confirmed with an hour.How's that for speed. ;-)

No Paperwork

Anyone, from any country, of any age can accept Bitcoins within minutes. There is no ID card, passport or proof of address that all conventional banks required to open an account.

Be Your Own Bank

You can literally have a “bank in your pocket” by way of a smartphone. This means that the bank is open 24/7, and you never have to ask permission to make a transaction of any kind.
